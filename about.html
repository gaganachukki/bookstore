Our History

How We Started

2023 - The Spark

Stackly began in a small garage in Seattle with just a curated list of 50 favorite books and a dream.

2024 - Going Digital

Launched our online platform, connecting independent authors directly with passionate readers.

2026 - Global Reach

Now shipping to over 40 countries, with a vibrant community of over 100,000 monthly active readers.
